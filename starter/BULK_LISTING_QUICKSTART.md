### 8. Troubleshooting

**"Add photos" error**: Must select at least some photos before publishing

**"Select a parish" error**: Parish is required in base data

**"At least one listing" error**: Need area + price in at least one row

**Wrong photo count**: System warns if insufficient photos, you can proceed with fewer

**Template not loading**: Templates saved in browser, clear cache if issues
